Signs You Need Roofing Work

Northeast Ohio weather takes a toll on roofs. If you notice any of these signs, it is time to call for a professional assessment.

  • Missing, cracked, or curling shingles
  • Water stains on ceilings or walls
  • Daylight visible through the roof deck
  • Sagging areas or visible wear
  • Your roof is 20+ years old
