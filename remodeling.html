Lead-Safe Remodeling

Interior remodeling in pre-1978 homes can disturb lead-based paint on walls, trim, and doors. As an EPA Lead-Safe Certified firm (RRP), we follow strict containment and cleanup protocols to protect your family throughout the renovation.
